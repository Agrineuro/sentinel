# Sentinel

> **Build a home for your digital world.**

Sentinel is an open-source AI operations platform for homelabs and self-hosted infrastructure.

Rather than replacing the tools you already use, Sentinel connects them into a single, intelligent operating environment. It combines infrastructure management, automation, artificial intelligence, monitoring, and personalization into one extensible platform that grows with you.

Whether you're running a single Raspberry Pi or a rack full of servers, Sentinel is designed to become the place you start your day—and the place your infrastructure comes alive.

---

## Project Status

> **Early Development**

Sentinel is currently in the planning and architecture phase.

The foundation is being built publicly from day one. While the codebase is just beginning, the long-term vision, architecture, and project principles are being established first to create a stable direction for future development.

If you're interested in helping shape the future of the project, now is the best time to get involved.

# Why Sentinel?

Modern homelabs have become incredibly powerful.

They've also become incredibly fragmented.

A typical environment may include:

* Virtualization platforms
* NAS appliances
* Linux and Windows systems
* Containers
* Smart home platforms
* Monitoring tools
* AI models
* Cameras
* Media servers
* Network appliances
* Automation platforms

Every one of them has its own dashboard.

Its own login.

Its own terminology.

Its own workflow.

Sentinel exists to unify them into one cohesive experience.

Not by replacing those tools—but by understanding how they work together.

---

# Core Goals

Sentinel is being built around a few simple ideas.

* **One platform** for your entire self-hosted environment.
* **Local-first** with no cloud dependency required.
* **Privacy-first** with complete user ownership of data.
* **AI that collaborates**, not just chats.
* **Plugin-first** architecture for limitless expansion.
* **API-first** design for automation and integration.
* **Beginner friendly** without limiting advanced users.
* **Community driven** and completely open source.

---

# The Experience

Sentinel should feel different.

It should feel like coming home.

Imagine opening your dashboard and seeing more than graphs.

Instead, Sentinel greets you with meaningful insight.

> Good morning.

> Your overnight backups completed successfully.

> Your photo library finished indexing.

> Two Docker containers have updates available.

> Your UPS battery health has declined slightly over the past month.

> Would you like me to update your services tonight after everyone has gone to bed?

The goal is to make infrastructure feel understandable, approachable, and alive.

# Design Philosophy

Sentinel follows a few core principles:

* Software should adapt to people—not the other way around.
* Privacy is a feature.
* Complexity should be optional.
* Intelligence should always be transparent.
* Every feature should respect user ownership.
* The platform should become more useful the longer you use it.

These ideas are expanded in the project's **FOUNDATION.md** document.
